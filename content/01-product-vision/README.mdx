# Act 1: Product Vision

FitMe is an iOS fitness platform that unifies training, nutrition, recovery, and body composition tracking into a single privacy-first experience. Where most fitness apps force users to juggle 3-5 separate tools and surrender their health data to the cloud, FitMe keeps everything encrypted on-device and uses federated AI to deliver population-level insights without ever exposing personal information.

What makes this project worth studying is not just the app itself, but the rigor behind every product decision. Every feature ships with a PRD, success metrics, kill criteria, and a scientific evidence base. The documents in this section show how a solo-developer project can operate with the discipline of a well-run product team --- from market positioning through formula derivation to notification intelligence.
