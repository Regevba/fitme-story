## Description (~1280 chars)

FitMe replaces your training log, meal tracker, and recovery dashboard with one app that knows what you should do today — without ever seeing your private health data.

FEATURES:
• 87 exercises with progressive overload tracking and automatic PR detection
• Dynamic macro targets that adapt to your training day and goals
• Readiness scoring powered by HRV, sleep, and heart rate
• AI coaching that learns your patterns while keeping data on-device
• Zero-knowledge encryption — your health data never leaves unencrypted

TRAINING:
Log sets and reps with one tap. FitMe detects personal records automatically and adjusts your next session based on recovery, not a rigid schedule. Rest timers, exercise swaps, and volume tracking all in one place.

NUTRITION:
Set macro targets once. FitMe adjusts them on training days vs rest days and tracks meals, supplements, and streaks. No barcode scanning required — smart search handles the heavy lifting.

RECOVERY:
Your readiness score synthesises HRV, resting heart rate, and sleep quality into a single number every morning. Green means go. Amber means modify. Red means rest.

PRIVACY FIRST:
AES-256 encryption, Secure Enclave key storage, federated AI. No data sold. Ever. FitMe is built for athletes who take their health data as seriously as their training.
